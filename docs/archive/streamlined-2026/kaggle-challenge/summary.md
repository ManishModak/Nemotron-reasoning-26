# NVIDIA Nemotron Model Reasoning Challenge (Kaggle)

**Overview:** A competition focused on advancing reasoning techniques using NVIDIA Nemotron open models on a novel benchmark developed by NVIDIA Research.

*   **Objective:** Improve reasoning accuracy of the **Nemotron-3-Nano-30B** base model.
*   **Key Tasks:** Participants experiment with prompting strategies, data filtering/curation, synthetic data generation, reinforcement learning, and lightweight fine-tuning (LoRA).
*   **Submission Requirement:** A compatible LoRA adapter (rank ≤ 32) for the Nemotron-3-Nano-30B model.
*   **Timeline:**
    *   **Start Date:** March 16, 2026
    *   **Entry/Team Merger Deadline:** June 8, 2026
    *   **Final Submission Deadline:** June 15, 2026
*   **Prizes:** Total prize pool of ~$106,388, including cash prizes (up to $25,000 for 1st place) and NVIDIA DGX Spark systems.

**Source:** [Kaggle Challenge Overview](https://www.kaggle.com/competitions/nvidia-nemotron-model-reasoning-challenge/overview)
