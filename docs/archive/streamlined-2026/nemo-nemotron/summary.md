# NVIDIA NeMo Nemotron

**Overview:** A developer asset hub providing a one-stop resource for the Nemotron family of models, including training recipes, deployment guides, and datasets.

*   **Model Tiers:**
    *   **Nano:** Optimized for edge and PC deployments.
    *   **Super:** High throughput for single GPU deployments.
    *   **Ultra:** Designed for multi-GPU datacenter applications.
*   **Key Features:** Open models with transparent training data, compute efficiency via TensorRT-LLM, and high accuracy for agentic workflows.
*   **Repository Structure:**
    *   `src/nemotron/recipes/`: Reproducible training pipelines (Pretrain → SFT → RL).
    *   `usage-cookbook/`: Jupyter notebooks for deployment and inference.
    *   `use-case-examples/`: End-to-end applications like RAG and agentic workflows.
*   **Datasets:** Includes a vast catalogue of open datasets for code, math, science, safety, and multimodal AI.

**Source:** [NVIDIA-NeMo/Nemotron GitHub](https://github.com/NVIDIA-NeMo/Nemotron)
